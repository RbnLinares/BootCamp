Treatment for Stress, Anxiety, and Depression

Stress Treatment

Here are some effective strategies to manage stress:

  • Mindfulness meditation
  • Regular physical activity
  • Adequate sleep
  • Healthy diet

For more information, visit:

  • Mayo Clinic - Stress Management

Anxiety Treatment

Consider these approaches to alleviate anxiety symptoms:

  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT)
  • Meditation and relaxation techniques
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Medication

Explore further resources:

  • NIMH - Anxiety Disorders

Depression Treatment

Strategies for coping with depression include:

  • Psychotherapy
  • Antidepressant medication
  • Lifestyle adjustments
  • Support groups

Learn more about depression treatment:

  • Mayo Clinic - Depression Treatment
